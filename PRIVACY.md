# Privacy Policy — ElementCSS

**Last updated:** March 16, 2026

## Data Collection

ElementCSS does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

## How It Works

- All CSS extraction runs **locally** in your browser's DevTools context.
- Extracted styles are copied to your **local clipboard** only.
- The extension makes **zero network requests** — no analytics, no telemetry, no external services.
- No data leaves your machine at any point.

## Permissions

- **clipboardWrite** — Used solely to copy extracted CSS to your clipboard when you click a button.

## Contact

For questions or concerns, open an issue at https://github.com/SpyrosBou/ElementCSS-to-clipboard/issues
